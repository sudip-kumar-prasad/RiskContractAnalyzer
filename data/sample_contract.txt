CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between
Acme Corporation ("Client") and LegalTech Solutions Ltd. ("Consultant").

1. SERVICES

The Consultant agrees to provide software consulting services as described in Schedule A attached hereto.
The Client agrees to pay the Consultant a fixed monthly retainer of $10,000 USD.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of twelve (12) months.
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice.
Client may terminate immediately for cause, with forfeiture of any retainer amounts paid in advance.

3. INDEMNIFICATION

Consultant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client and its officers, directors, employees,
and agents from and against any and all claims, liabilities, damages, penalties, and expenses
arising out of or related to Consultant's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
The total liability of Consultant under this Agreement shall not exceed the amounts paid in the
preceding three (3) months. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Consultant assumes unlimited liability
for breaches of confidentiality obligations.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product created by Consultant under this Agreement shall be irrevocable, perpetual,
and royalty-free. Client shall have full assignment and transfer of rights to all deliverables,
including any sublicense rights. Consultant hereby waives any moral rights to such work product.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary information and
trade secrets. Consultant shall not disclose any confidential information to third parties without
prior written consent. This obligation shall survive termination of the Agreement.

7. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term and for a period of two (2) years following termination, Consultant agrees to a
non-solicitation and non-compete provision restricting Consultant from providing services to
Client's direct competitors. This constitutes a restraint of trade enforceable under governing law.

8. PAYMENT TERMS

Client shall pay all invoices within thirty (30) days of receipt. Late payment shall accrue
compound interest at a rate of 1.5% per month. A surcharge of 5% shall be applied to amounts
outstanding beyond sixty (60) days. Client may not withhold or deduct any amounts from payments.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively through binding arbitration
under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The parties hereby waive any right to
jury trial. The arbitral award shall be final and binding under the jurisdiction of New York.

10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. Any amendments must be in
writing. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York. Funds of $50,000
shall be held in escrow pending completion of milestones. Client may deduct milestone shortfalls
from final payment.

11. STANDARD DELIVERY TERMS

Consultant will make reasonable commercial efforts to deliver services on time.
The Consultant team will maintain professional conduct throughout the engagement.
Regular status updates will be provided via email every two weeks.
Both parties agree to cooperate in good faith to resolve any operational issues.
